VISION
Sara Roy is a highly creative fashion stylist and graphic designer based in Milan, Italy. By blending advanced editorial composition with avant-garde styling concepts, she translates textile draping and silhouette lines into strong personal and cultural statements. Her work builds bridges between visual forms and modern design identity.
